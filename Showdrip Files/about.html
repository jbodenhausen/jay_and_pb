About

Choose your own adventure!

    

I used to find myself frustrated by how much time it took to find musical entertainment near me that I would actually be interested in watching. The sources I used to find music were local periodicals, online listings for periodicals, ticket vendors’ websites, or other event websites. These sources all have major downsides. Listings in hard copies of periodicals are often scattered in no apparent order and are printed in many different fonts and styles making it slow and chaotic to find out what is happening. Online listings are an improvement over this, however, these are often too granular and require you to search through a multitude of listings just to find the small subset of shows you might like. Ticket vendors’ websites will often only highlight the biggest shows or require you to go to other vendor/event sites to find out what's playing. To solve these problems, Showdrip was created to allow users to quickly search shows in an area, get basic information about venues, and provide listings that clearly highlight venue and band names. On Showdrip users are also able to set their favorite venues allowing them to easily get an idea of what's going on at the places they enjoy.
